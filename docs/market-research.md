# Swiss Invoicing Software — Market Research

_Date: 2026-03-08_

## Competitive Landscape

### 1. Magic Heidi

- **URL**: https://magicheidi.ch/de
- **Target**: Solo freelancers, self-employed
- **Languages**: DE, FR, IT, EN
- **Swiss QR bill**: Yes, SIX-compliant
- **Key features**: Invoice creation with QR codes, AI receipt scanning, expense tracking, multi-currency (50+), Swiss VAT (effective + lump-sum), bank import (UBS, PostFinance, Raiffeisen, ZKB), quotes, customer management, real-time P&L, tax calculator. Native apps on iOS, Android, macOS, Windows, web.
- **Pricing**:
  - Free: 3 invoices, 3 expenses (perpetual)
  - Monthly: CHF 30/month
  - Annual: CHF 24.99/month (CHF 299/year)
- **Data hosting**: Zurich, Switzerland

### 2. bexio

- **URL**: https://www.bexio.com/en-CH/packages-and-prices
- **Target**: SMEs of all sizes (30,000+ customers)
- **Languages**: DE, FR, IT, EN
- **Swiss QR bill**: Yes
- **Key features**: Full ERP — invoicing, accounting, CRM, payroll, inventory, e-banking (44+ Swiss banks), project management, 90-100+ marketplace apps, AI document processing (Kontera).
- **Pricing** (annual billing):
  - Basic: CHF 35/month (1 user)
  - Advanced: CHF 42/month (2 users)
  - Optima: CHF 69/month (5 users)
  - Ultimate: CHF 119/month (25 users)
- **Data hosting**: Switzerland

### 3. smallinvoice

- **URL**: https://www.smallinvoice.ch/en/
- **Target**: SMEs, startups, freelancers
- **Languages**: DE, FR, IT, ES, EN
- **Swiss QR bill**: Yes
- **Key features**: Invoicing, CRM, expense management, project management, time tracking, PostFinance integration, accounting exports (Banana, INFONIQA, Topal), postal mail dispatch.
- **Pricing**:
  - Free: Up to CHF 70,000 cumulative invoicing (no QR bills, no time tracking)
  - Starter: ~CHF 15/month
  - Professional: ~CHF 30/month (3 users)
  - Business: ~CHF 45/month (5 users)

### 4. HelvetiBill

- **URL**: https://helvetibill.ch
- **Target**: Freelancers, sole proprietors, agencies
- **Languages**: German only
- **Swiss QR bill**: Yes, SIX-compliant
- **Key features**: Lead management, quote pipeline, QR-bill invoicing, credit notes, recurring invoices, customer database, customer portal, business dashboard, VAT calculation.
- **Pricing**:
  - 7-day free trial
  - Pro: CHF 12/month (20 customers, 10 invoices/month)
  - Pro+: CHF 24/month (unlimited)

### 5. ToBill

- **URL**: https://tobill.ch
- **Target**: Freelancers, SMEs, fiduciaries
- **Languages**: DE, FR
- **Swiss QR bill**: Yes
- **Key features**: Invoicing, AI expense scanning, Swiss VAT preconfigured (8.1%, 2.6%, 3.8%), multi-currency with daily exchange rates, bank reconciliation, fiduciary reporting, data import from bexio/Magic Heidi.
- **Pricing**:
  - Starter: Free (limited)
  - Pro: CHF 25/month

### 6. Infinity.swiss

- **URL**: https://www.infinity.swiss
- **Target**: German-speaking Swiss freelancers
- **Languages**: German only
- **Swiss QR bill**: Yes
- **Key features**: Invoicing, AI expense categorization (90%+ accuracy), automated double-entry bookkeeping, multi-rate VAT, bank connection.
- **Pricing**:
  - Start: CHF 24/month (1 bank account, 2 users)
  - Pro: CHF 38/month (full VAT accounting, analytics)
- **Limitations**: Web only, German only

### 7. MILKEE

- **URL**: https://milkee.ch
- **Target**: German-speaking Swiss freelancers under CHF 100,000 turnover
- **Languages**: German only
- **Swiss QR bill**: Yes
- **Key features**: Client/project management, time tracking, QR bill creation, income-expense accounting, 30-day financial forecast, tax + AHV calculation. Mobile apps.
- **Pricing**:
  - Standard: CHF 30/month (or CHF 300/year)
  - 7-day free trial

### 8. Swiss21 (by Abacus)

- **URL**: https://swiss21.org/en/
- **Target**: Self-employed, SMEs, startups, associations
- **Languages**: DE, FR
- **Swiss QR bill**: Yes
- **Key features**: Full suite — invoicing (21.Invoice), accounting (21.AbaNinja with 70+ bank connections), CRM, e-commerce, POS, payroll, mobile expense reporting (AbaClik with AI). E-Bill support.
- **Pricing**:
  - Free plan: Small volume
  - From CHF 21/month

### 9. KLARA (Swiss Post)

- **URL**: https://www.klara.ch/en/
- **Target**: SMEs, startups, small businesses
- **Languages**: DE, FR, IT, EN
- **Swiss QR bill**: Yes
- **Key features**: Invoicing, accounting, CRM, payroll — all core services free. Modular and scalable.
- **Pricing**:
  - Core services: Free
  - Business PLUS: from CHF 35/month
  - Inventory add-on: CHF 29/month
- **Note**: Owned by Swiss Post. Reports of reliability issues (42 bug reports in 2024, 3-day support delays)

### 10. Cresus

- **URL**: https://cresus.ch
- **Target**: SMEs, freelancers (strong in Romandie)
- **Languages**: DE, FR
- **Swiss QR bill**: Yes
- **Key features**: Modular — Faktura (invoicing), Comptabilite (accounting), Banking, Salaires (payroll). Inventory, VAT statements.
- **Pricing**:
  - Rental: from CHF 20/month
  - Purchase: from CHF 600 one-time (+ CHF 260/year updates)
- **Limitations**: Desktop-only, dated UI

### 11. pebe Live

- **URL**: https://www.pebelive.ch
- **Target**: Small businesses, startups, tradespeople, freelancers
- **Languages**: German only
- **Key features**: Invoicing, automated bookkeeping, balance sheet/income statement, payroll, creditor management, REST API, 2FA. Accountant collaboration portal.
- **Pricing**: Free version available; paid plans not publicly listed
- **Data hosting**: Zurich and Geneva

### 12. qr-rechnung.net (free tool)

- **URL**: https://qr-rechnung.net/
- **Target**: Anyone needing a quick QR bill
- **Features**: Free QR bill creation and scanning, no registration
- **Pricing**: Free
- **Limitations**: QR bill generation only — not a full invoicing solution

## Market Gap Analysis

### eRechnung Advantages

- **Multi-language** (DE/EN/ES/FR/IT) — most budget tools are German-only
- **Dual letter standards** (SN 010130 + DIN 5008) — unique differentiator, no competitor offers both
- **Swiss QR bill** built-in
- **Lightweight, modern stack** (Vue 3, Vite, Tailwind) — fast, no legacy baggage
- **No backend dependency** — potential for offline-first / privacy-focused positioning

### The Gap

- Multi-language tools start at CHF 25+/mo (Magic Heidi, smallinvoice)
- Budget tools (CHF 12–24) are all German-only
- Free tools (KLARA, Swiss21) are feature-heavy but clunky and unreliable

## Required Features to Compete (MVP)

### Must-haves (before any paid tier)

1. **Persistent data** — database or localStorage (currently static JSON)
2. **Client management** — CRUD for customers
3. **PDF export** — reliable server-side rendering (not browser print)
4. **Invoice numbering** — automatic sequential numbering
5. **User accounts** — authentication + data isolation

### Nice-to-haves (for Pro tier)

- Bank reconciliation (CSV import minimum)
- Expense tracking
- Swiss VAT calculation (8.1%, 2.6%, 3.8%)
- Recurring invoices
- Email sending from app
- Dashboard with revenue overview
